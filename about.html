About me

Hello, welcome to my About Me page. Just call me Alex.

I am is a highly self-motivated person who enjoys learning and taking on new challenges. For the past five years I have worked as a professional civil servant at the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection Ghana. Within these years I have held the position of an Assistant Director at the Finance and Administration Department and the Social Protection Directorate for three and two years respectively. I have a proven record of successful implementation of initiative driven projects both at local and national level.
